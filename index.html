About SIOA

The Society of Indian Origami Artists stands as India's premier institution for the art and craft of paper folding. We are committed to establishing origami as a recognized cultural practice, fostering creativity, precision, and artistic expression through this timeless medium.

SIOA serves as a unifying force for origami practitioners across the nation, providing a structured platform for learning, competition, and artistic excellence. About National Origami Competition

The National Origami Competition represents the pinnacle of competitive paper folding in India. This annual event brings together the nation's most skilled practitioners to demonstrate technical excellence, artistic vision, and creative innovation.

Participants compete across multiple categories, each designed to test different aspects of origami mastery‚Äîfrom classical forms and complex modular designs to original creations and thematic interpretations. Our judging criteria emphasize precision, creativity, and cultural significance.

Winners receive official certificates, trophies, and national-level recognition, establishing their standing within India's origami community.
