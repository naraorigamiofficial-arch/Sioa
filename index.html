About

Society of Indian Origami Artists

The Society of Indian Origami Artists stands as India's premier institution for the art of paper folding. We are committed to establishing origami as a recognized cultural practice, fostering creativity, precision, and artistic expression through this timeless medium.

SIOA serves as a unifying force for origami practitioners across the nation, providing a structured platform for learning, competition, and artistic excellence. Our mission extends beyond recreation, we cultivate discipline, spatial thinking, and cultural appreciation through the meditative practice of origami.

As a national-level organization, we uphold the highest standards of artistic integrity and competitive fairness, ensuring that origami is celebrated as both an art form and a competitive discipline worthy of institutional recognition.

Our Platform

What We Offer

National Competitions

Structured, merit-based competitions that recognize technical skill, creativity, and mastery. Our events set the benchmark for competitive origami in India.

Educational Resources

Comprehensive training materials, workshops, and certification programs designed to elevate practitioners from novice to master level.

Community Network

A nationwide network of artists, educators, and enthusiasts united by a shared dedication to excellence in origami.

Cultural Preservation

Safeguarding traditional techniques while encouraging innovation, ensuring origami remains a living, evolving art form.

Recognition & Awards

Official recognition for outstanding artists and contributors, celebrating excellence and inspiring the next generation of origami masters.

Research & Innovation

Advancing the mathematical, artistic, and pedagogical dimensions of origami through scholarly research and creative exploration.

Competition

National Origami Competition

The National Origami Competition (VAGH 2026), represents the pinnacle of competitive paper folding in India. This annual event brings together the nation's most skilled practitioners to demonstrate technical excellence, artistic vision, and creative innovation.

Participants compete across multiple categories, each designed to test different aspects of origami mastery, from classical forms and complex designs to original creations and thematic interpretations. Our judging criteria emphasize precision, creativity, and cultural significance.

National Recognition

Winners receive official certificates, trophies, and national-level recognition, establishing their standing within India's origami community.

Multiple Categories

Age-based divisions and skill-level categories ensure fair competition and provide opportunities for practitioners at every stage of their journey.

Expert Evaluation

A distinguished panel of master folders and cultural experts ensures transparent, merit-based judging aligned with international standards.

Cultural Impact

Beyond competition, this event celebrates origami as a cultural practice, fostering public appreciation and inspiring future generations.

Educational Workshops

Concurrent workshops led by renowned artists provide learning opportunities and skill development for all participants.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with fellow artists, educators, and enthusiasts, building relationships that extend beyond the competition.
